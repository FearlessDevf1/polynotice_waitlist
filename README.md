# PolyNotice Waitlist

**PolyNotice** is a prediction market monitoring, intelligence, and automation platform for Polymarket and beyond.

Traders currently lack tools for real time alerts, risk management, and actionable insights. PolyNotice bridges that gap by giving users visibility, alerts, analytics, and future non-custodial automation, all without giving up custody of their funds.

## What is PolyNotice?

PolyNotice bridges the gap between prediction market traders and actionable intelligence. It provides:

- **Real-time monitoring** of markets and wallet activity
- **Advanced alerts** across multiple conditions
- **Market intelligence** with conviction heatmaps and trader tracking
- **Non-custodial automation** (coming soon) for risk management

## Features

### Free Tier
- Price alerts
- Trade monitoring (your connected wallet)
- Account monitoring (other traders)
- Portfolio visibility
- Multi-condition alerts
- Event impact alerts
- Basic conviction heatmap

### Premium & Premium+ (Coming Soon)
- Risk management (TP/SL)
- Trade automation
- Advanced intelligence
- And more...
