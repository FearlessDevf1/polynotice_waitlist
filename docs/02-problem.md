# The Problem

Prediction markets such as Polymarket offer powerful ways to trade on real-world events, but lack essential tools for active traders.

Current limitations include:

- No native price or multi-condition alerts
- No take-profit or stop-loss functionality
- Manual trade monitoring
- No visibility into top-performing traders
- No structured way to discover market opportunities

As a result, traders:
- Miss profitable exits
- React too late to market movements
- Lack insight into where smart money is flowing

PolyNotice addresses these gaps by introducing structured monitoring, intelligence, and automation.
