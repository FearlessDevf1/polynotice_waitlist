# Getting Started

PolyNotice is currently in early access.

This guide explains how to get started once access is available.

---

## 1. Create an Account

Sign up using your email to access the PolyNotice dashboard.

No wallet connection is required at this stage for basic usage.

---

## 2. Connect Your Wallet (Optional)

To enable trade monitoring:

- Connect your wallet (e.g. MetaMask, WalletConnect)
- Sign a message to verify ownership (no transaction required)

PolyNotice only reads wallet activity.  
It does not have access to your funds.

---

## 3. Track Your Trades

Once connected, PolyNotice will:

- Detect your open positions
- Display your active trades
- Track performance across markets

You can also monitor other wallets by entering their address.

---

## 4. Create Alerts

Set alerts based on your preferences:

- Price thresholds
- Percentage movement
- Multi-condition triggers (price + volume, etc.)

When conditions are met, you will receive a notification.

---

## 5. Receive Notifications

Alerts are delivered through:

- Email (default)
- Additional channels (planned)

Make sure notifications are enabled so you don't miss important updates.

---

## 6. Explore Market Intelligence

Use PolyNotice to discover opportunities:

- View the Conviction Heatmap
- Track high-activity markets
- Monitor top-performing traders

This helps you understand where capital and attention are flowing.

---

## 7. Automation (Coming Soon)

Future versions of PolyNotice will support:

- Take-profit and stop-loss automation
- Non-custodial execution
- Trade mirroring

All automation features will be:
- Optional
- Permission-based
- Non-custodial

---

## Notes

- PolyNotice does not provide financial advice
- Users are responsible for their own trades
- Features may evolve as the platform develops
