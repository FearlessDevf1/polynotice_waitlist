# Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Monitoring Foundation
- Wallet trade tracking
- Market data indexing
- Price alerts
- Email notifications
- Basic dashboard

Goal: Establish reliable monitoring infrastructure.

---

## Phase 2 — Advanced Alerts & Intelligence
- Multi-condition alerts
- Account monitoring
- Portfolio tracking
- Event impact alerts
- Market opportunity scanner

Goal: Provide actionable insights beyond basic alerts.

---

## Phase 3 — Market Intelligence Layer
- Conviction Heatmap (basic + advanced)
- Profit leader tracking
- Trader analytics
- Smart market discovery tools

Goal: Help users understand where capital and conviction are flowing.

---

## Phase 4 — Automation Layer
- Take-profit / stop-loss
- Non-custodial execution
- Partial exits
- Execution confirmations

Goal: Enable users to act without being online.

---

## Phase 5 — Advanced Automation & Expansion
- Trade mirroring
- AI agents (reward farming, strategies)
- Social signals
- Cross-market support

Goal: Transform PolyNotice into a full trading intelligence and automation platform.

Disclaimer

This roadmap reflects our current direction and priorities. Features and timelines may evolve based on user feedback and platform constraints.
