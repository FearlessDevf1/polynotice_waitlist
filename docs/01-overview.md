# PolyNotice

PolyNotice is a monitoring, intelligence, and automation platform for prediction markets, starting with Polymarket.

Prediction markets currently lack essential trading infrastructure such as alerts, risk management tools, and actionable intelligence. PolyNotice fills this gap by providing real-time monitoring, advanced alerts, and — in later phases — non-custodial automation.

PolyNotice is designed for traders who want visibility, better decision-making, and execution control without giving up custody of their funds.

## What PolyNotice Enables

- Monitor trades and wallets in real time
- Set advanced alerts across markets
- Track top-performing traders
- Discover high-conviction opportunities
- Automate trade exits (planned)

PolyNotice evolves from a simple alert system into a full prediction market intelligence layer.
